🎉 Congratulations to Dr. Lakhvir Kaur for winning the 2026 Outstanding Dissertation Award in the field of School Climate

<!--more-->

Her dissertation is entitled: "Cultural Congruity and School Climate: A Cross-Cultural Validation of the School Cultural Congruity Scale (SCCS)". Her dissertation was chaired by Dr. Shane Jimerson. 

She is a graduate of the school psychology program at UC Santa Barbara's Department of Counseling, Clinical, and School Psychology.
