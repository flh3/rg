The School Community, Climate, and Culture (SC3) Special Interest Group (SIG) of the American Educational Research Association (AERA) is soliciting applications for the 2026 Outstanding  Dissertation Award in the Field of School Climate. This award acknowledges an exemplary contribution  to research in school climate with outstanding scientific or scholarly significance, quality of study methodology, and likelihood to impact the field. The award comes with a $500 honorarium. The award will be announced prior to the AERA Annual Meeting.

## Eligibility 

Applicants must have received their Ph.D. or Ed.D. after January 1, 2024 or intend to defend their dissertation not later than May 31, 2026. Applications must be made by the individual applying for the award. The submitted written material must be a dissertation. Applicants must be members of AERA, but it is not a requirement that they be members of the SC3 SIG. This is a nonresidential award, and international applicants are encouraged to apply.
